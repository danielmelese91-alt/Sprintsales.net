About Sprintsales

We Build the Sales Systems Businesses Wish They Had Earlier

Sprintsales started from a simple observation: many strong Ethiopian businesses have real products, real customers, and real ambition, but their digital presence does not yet support the way modern buyers discover and decide.

Based in Addis Ababa
Focus Digital sales infrastructure

Our story

From Marketing Ideas to Operating Systems

We are building Sprintsales as a practical growth partner: not just a design agency, not just a social media team, but a group that connects website, social commerce, customer service automation, and team training into one working system.

What we believe

  • Good digital systems should be understandable by the business owner.
  • Automation should support human sales teams, not replace trust.
  • Design should make buying easier, not just make pages look expensive.
  • Every handover should leave the client more capable than before.
